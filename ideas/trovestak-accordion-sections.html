Q Can this drive an external monitor? I need dual 4K displays for my home office setup.
A
The MacBook Air M4 supports one external display at up to 6K/60Hz when the lid is closed (clamshell mode). With the lid open it mirrors or extends to one external display. For true dual-monitor use, you'd need a MacBook Pro or a USB-C dock that splits the signal (some limitations apply).

Q Is 256GB storage enough, or should I upgrade to 512GB?
A
It depends on your usage. 256GB is fine if you use iCloud Drive for photos/documents and stream media. If you store large video projects, raw photos, or many heavy apps locally — go 512GB. Storage cannot be upgraded after purchase, so err on the side of more if unsure.

Q Is the Midnight finish prone to fingerprints and scratches?
A
Midnight does show fingerprints more than Silver or Starlight — though Apple's anodization process reduces it vs previous dark finishes. A microfiber cloth cleans it instantly. Scratches are rare with normal use; the aluminum is quite durable. Many prefer a slim case for peace of mind.
